How it works

Your goals. Your pace.
Zero excuses.

BFF uses AI to track your diet, movement, and habits — then holds you accountable the way only a real best friend can.

Pick your BFF

Choose a persona that matches your vibe — Bro, Bestie, Queen, or Ride or Die. Each has a totally different coaching style.

Set your goals

Tell BFF what you're working toward. Weight loss, muscle gain, better nutrition — it builds a real plan around your actual life.

Track daily

Log your meals, movement, and moods. BFF pays attention so you don't have to think about the details — just show up.

Stay accountable

BFF checks in, calls you out when you slip, and celebrates your wins — every single day. No sugarcoating. Just results.
